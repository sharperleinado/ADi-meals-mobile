Our story



ADi meals Limited was first registered as a business name “ADi meals, BN 8073” in the year 2020 to venture into Food and Beverages. We wanted to start out as a Snack Drink company. A company whose sole purpose was to produce snack and drink together in a container which would serve as an easy meal to our consumers. Our goal has always been to provide easy meals to our consumers, but as a snack drink project we were embarking upon, we did not have enough funding to begin with so, we decided to dissect our goals and digress our business into opening a physical restaurant in Akure.

In 2021, we opened a restaurant in irese area of Akure, Ondo state. We began by selling food like Egusi and Pounded yam, White rice and beans, Jollof rice, and many more meals. Business was booming but along the line, things became sour, and our operations folded up in July which was as a result of our dependency on a little community in the whole of Akure to scale our business.

Today, we have rebranded ourselves as a Limited company to give us a better reputation. We have re-strategize our operations not to focus on operating a physical restaurant but to operate online by having a digital market place on the web through which our customers can order food online, thereby reaching a larger audience.
