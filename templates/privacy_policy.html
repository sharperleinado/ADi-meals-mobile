PRIVACY POLICY


Adi Meals Limited (“Adimeals,” “we,” “our,” or “us”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal data in compliance with the Nigeria Data Protection Act (NDPA). By accessing or using our platform, you consent to the practices described in this policy. If you have any questions, please contact us at adimeals@gmail.com.

  1. Information We Collect


    1. Personal Information Provided by You:

      1. Full name, email address, phone number, and delivery address.

      2. Payment information (e.g., card details for transactions).


    2. Automatically Collected Information:

      1. Device information (IP address, browser type, etc.).

      2. Usage data (pages visited, time spent on the platform, etc.).


    3. Third-party Data:

      1. If you connect through third-party services (e.g., Google, Facebook), we may collect data from those accounts in accordance with their privacy policies.

  2. How We Use Your Information

    1. To fulfill and deliver your orders.

    2. To manage your account and provide customer support.

    3. To send notifications about your orders and updates about our services.

    4. For marketing and promotional activities (only with your consent).

    5. To comply with legal and regulatory obligations.


  3. Legal Basis for Processing

    1. Your consent for specific activities (e.g., promotional messages).

    2. Contractual necessity to fulfill your orders and provide services.

    3. Legal compliance with applicable regulations.


  4. Data Retention

    1. We retain your personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined or as required by law. For inactive accounts, personal data may be deleted after 3 months of inactivity.


  5. Sharing of Information

    1. We may share your information with:

      1. Service Providers: For payment processing, delivery services, or analytics.

      2. Regulatory Authorities: If required by law.

      3. Third Parties: Only with your consent or as legally mandated.


    2. We ensure that any third-party processors comply with NDPA requirements. For example, our payment processors are required to adhere to stringent security standards to protect your information.

  6. Data Security

    1. We implement robust technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, or loss. These measures include encryption for data transmission, secure storage solutions, and access control protocols.


  7. Your Rights

    1. Under the NDPA, you have the following rights:

      1. To access your personal data.

      2. To rectify inaccurate data.

      3. To withdraw consent at any time (where applicable).

      4. To object to processing for direct marketing purposes.

      5. To request deletion of your data (“right to be forgotten”).

      6. To lodge a complaint with the Nigerian Data Protection Commission if you believe your rights are violated.


  8. Cookies

    1. We use cookies to improve user experience, such as for storing preferences and enhancing site navigation. You may manage your cookie settings through your browser preferences or the cookie management tools available on our platform.

    2. Some third-party services used on our platform may also set cookies. We encourage you to review their privacy policies for more details on their cookie usage.


  9. Third-party Links

    1. Our platform may include links to third-party websites, such as social media platforms or external services. We are not responsible for their privacy practices. We encourage you to review their privacy policies before sharing any personal information on those platforms.


  10. Changes to This Policy

    1. We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. You will be notified of significant changes via the platform or email. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your data.


  11. Surveys

    We may occasionally request your participation in surveys to gain insights into your experience with our Services and identify areas for improvement. If you choose to participate, we may collect demographic information such as age, marital status, ethnicity, profession, and income, which may include sensitive data. We may also collect

    feedback on how you use our Services and your overall experience. By participating in any survey, you consent to our collection and use of the information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.


  12. Governing Law

    This Privacy Policy is governed by the Nigeria Data Protection Act (NDPA) 2023, and other applicable data protection laws in Nigeria. In the event that any provision of this policy is inconsistent with a law, regulation, or convention, the relevant law, regulation, or convention shall prevail.


    This Privacy Policy is subject to the laws of the Federal Republic of Nigeria. It applies to all users of Adi Meals’ platform and anyone who contacts or submits information to Adi Meals.
