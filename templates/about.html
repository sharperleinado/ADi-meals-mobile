Story Story!

Hi {% if user.is_authenticated %} {{fname|capfirst}}, {% else %} Prospective Customer, {% endif %} my name is ADi, and I would love to share you a part of my story on what became the inception of ADi meals as a Soup and Food service company.

On the morning of February 27th, in 2006, a big decision was taken in my family by my Mum, whom I will refer to her as Mama ADi in the rest of my story. She decided to part ways with her then husband, which made her leave Lagos with my little bro to settle down in Akure, leaving behind myself and two other siblings.

Few weeks down the line, I and my siblings got abandoned at my Aunt's place where we lived for a year plus without having to attend school. Although, we attended summer and spring lessons during the holidays. Mum on the other end was struggling really hard to raise enough money to migrate us from Lagos to Akure.

Then, one afternoon, a day to always remember, I was at my Aunt's shop when I saw my mum at the other side of the road. I couldn't believe my eyes until she was right beside me. This was a woman that looked so slim before she had left us to looking chubby, but that's a story for another day. We didn't know she had planned a surprise visit with our aunt initially. To cut the long story short, we were in the first bus to Akure the following week.

Part 2 (Intersection)

On arriving to Akure in year 2007, the life we met there is a story for another day. Mama ADi always had a thought of how she was going to survive alone with four kids, but God did!

Though, technically she wasn't alone. It would be of no good of me as the story teller if I did not make mention of my Uncle, Igor. He deserves an accolade as the best uncle in the world. I would dedicate another day to tell you about how good and supporting he has been to us.

Now, back to my story; She started off as a cook, where she specialized in catering services for events, soup and food services for people. One unique thing she does before leaving the house for catering jobs was to ensure she prepared enough soup in small and medium containers that would last us while she was away. These were soups that would last 3-5days refigerated.

But over the years, Mama ADi had been undervalued and underpaid despite the so much efforts she puts into cooking. There were times she would return from a catering job that lasted about 5days, and she would be unable to see properly for the next two days due to the furnace of fire she had endured during work. I remember I would go to the backyard of the house to cry my ass out because I wanted to change my mum's story.

So, fastforward to after I graduated University, I thought of how I could use the power of coding, the power of creating applications to change Mama ADi's business for good, since it was part of what I studied back in the university. I gathered the best brains, and together we built an application where people can place order for Mama ADi's food or soup in various box sizes which is where we are today. Her dream has always been to produce containerized food and soups for Nigerians. Today, we are in a world where everyone is busy. ADi meals hopes to simplify food and soup cooking by exchanging our busy hours with quality meals. Imagine a lady Banker that closes from work by 6pm but would still have to be home to cook for the family or a Teacher or Bachelor that closes from work by 4pm but would still have to go home to cook, when it can be ordered from us.

Sometimes, we understand that Nigerians can be calculative in spending, for example: I have a family of 6 in the house, rather than buying containerized food for all six, a Nigerian woman might decide to cook at home but this is what our soup service hopes to solve by offering you your choice of soup, and then you choose to prepare the chewable food at his or her own convenience. That way, you have killed a bed with two stones. We have saved your time, money, and even reduced the stress it would take you to cook the soup.

You see, my dear friend, everything you have read so far is not to score some emotional cheap point but to categorically inform you that you would not be wasting your money when you buy any of our meals. I beat my chest to tell you that Mama ADi cooks the best soups, and food in Nigeria! If you eventually buy our soup or food, and you feel any regrets, I will not only refund you but tender an apology by trekking down to your home to deliver the apology myself!

Please, help Mama ADi, and ADi meals win today by buying our meals.
